Usefulness

Our application is useful because as students we know the struggle that comes with buying and selling books. You don't know if you can trust others to give you the best price for a book, and buy/selling from the bookstore is just plain stupid (they will leave you pennyless). Yes, there are a few sites that let students buy and sell books but even then, it's hard to tell without doing work on your own if the same book can be bought elsewhere for a cheaper price. Our application will allow students to buy/sell books at fair and competitive prices by allowing both the buyer and the seller to compare prices of the book online at other sites. In essence we want to do something similar to craiglist but specific to books only and allow the similar price comparison that sites like Kayak offer for plane tickets. As far as we know there is no other site out there that combines the functionality of craigslist and Kayak specifically for book selling/buying.

Realness

Our data will come from various other book buying/selling sites. We plan to look into Google Scholar, Chegg, and Amazon as potential sources for price comparison.
